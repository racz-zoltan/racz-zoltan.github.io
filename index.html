Progressive Web App (PWA) for Seamless Integration and Accessibility

CarryPass offers unparalleled flexibility and accessibility, allowing you to manage your passwords seamlessly across all your devices. With its PWA, you can enjoy the convenience of accessing your passwords from any browser, eliminating the need for multiple applications or extensions. Say goodbye to device restrictions and hello to effortless password management on the go.

Offline Mode for Password Security

Whether you're online or offline, CarryPass ensures uninterrupted access to your passwords whenever you need them. Thanks to advanced caching and service worker technology, your passwords are secure, because they are not stored and still readily available even when you're offline.

How a Privacy-Focused Password Manager App Mitigates Privacy Concerns and Protects User Data

Privacy is not just a feature – it's the foundation. The privacy-focused approach of CarryPass ensures that your information remains strictly confidential and its zero-knowledge architecture safeguards your passwords from prying eyes. When you create a password, instead of sending the actual master password to a service provider or storing it on servers, the password is transformed in your browser using a one-way cryptographic hash function with key-stretching algorithm, and neither the master password nor the created password ever leave your device.

Enhancing Privacy with AES 256 Encryption

CarryPass takes privacy seriously by implementing robust AES 256 encryption for both text and file storage. AES 256 encryption is recognized as one of the most secure encryption standards available today, ensuring that your sensitive data remains protected from unauthorized access. With AES 256 encryption, your texts and files are encrypted using a symmetric key algorithm, meaning that only authorized users with the correct key can decrypt and access the information. This ensures that even if your data were somehow stolen from you, it would be virtually impossible for anyone without the encryption key to decipher its contents. Beyond just security, AES 256 encryption also enhances privacy by providing an additional layer of confidentiality. Your encrypted data remains indecipherable to anyone, including service providers or hackers, ensuring that your digital identity and sensitive information are kept private at all times.
