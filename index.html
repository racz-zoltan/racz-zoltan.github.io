Privacy by Default

Want to start the application?

CarryPass is a stateless and deterministic password manager.
It doesn’t store your data online — everything stays in your browser.
Your passwords are generated on demand from your personal key and settings, so the same inputs always produce the same results.

Create your personal key for this application.
Choose a passphrase and an icon sequence. These protect your generated passwords and locally stored settings. That’s why the stronger your personal key, the stronger your protection.

It’s recommended to use a random passphrase with at least 16 characters, or a diceware-style phrase with 8 or more words.
There’s no backup or recovery, so be sure to remember it.

Type your masterpassword

Tap Your Icon Sequence

Select 6 icons in any order you like. Tap again to deselect.
