Why Choose CarryPass?

Offline First

CarryPass can work entirely offline. No data collection, no cloud dependency, no sensitive-data transfer.

Private by Design

No accounts. No tracking. No ads. No subscriptions. Everything runs locally in your browser or device.

Secure

Your passwords and protected files remain under your control. No data is stored on remote servers.

Cross-platform

Its progressive web app design ensures that every feature works on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Expect a seamless experience no matter which operating system you use.

Universal

CarryPass works on your phone, tablet, or desktop — no installation needed, but installable with one tap. Use it anywhere, anytime, fully offline.

Business-friendly

CarryPass can be shared with your employees on any device, without setting up accounts. It works online or offline, installs easily, and keeps passwords out of the cloud.
