Main features

  • Security

    CarryPass employs enterprise-grade encryption (256-bit AES) on usernames, email addresses, passwords and private notes. The master password is further protected with a key stretching algorithm (PBKDF2_HMAC) with at least 300.000 iterations.

  • Privacy

    CarryPass keeps your data private. Usernames, passwords and notes are concealed and stored on your own portable device, so you can retain control over the information. Not only does another party not know, but also not have the information you value.

  • Anonimity

    Encrypted usernames, email addresses and notes can keep you non-identifiable even in case some of the stored information is revealed, such as the login URLs of the websites. Anonimity online is, of course, a completely different matter.

  • Two factor authentication

    Time-based one time password, as a second factor of authentication, together with your master password can further protect the information stored by the application.

  • Trust scaling

    Depending on how much you trust each device where you use CarryPass, you can control idle timeout of the application, and require time-based one time password for login.

  • Login automation

    Handles the login process for both desktop and web applications. No additional browser extension or plugin is required as CarryPass manages the login process on the screen.

CarryPass manages your strong passwords

CarryPass is the free offline password manager for those who need extra privacy, with 256-bit AES encryption and time-based one time password as a second factor of authentication. It is a small, lightweight password manager written fully in Python, using the Kivy framework. This offline password manager only stores your data on your device. The pwned password check needs internet connection, but plaintext passwords never leave your computer. See pwnedpassword for details. Since the usernames, email addresses, passwords and private notes are stored on your device, you have total control over their security. Whether the security, privacy and anonimity features of the application provide sufficient protection depends on your security threat landscape. See Pycryptodome - AES encryption, and the pbkdf2_hmac algorithm for details.

List of features

  • Portability across desktop devices

  • Login credential management: strong password generation and encryption, pwned password detection

  • Trust scaling across devices: idle timeout control, time-based one time password

  • Convenience features: login automation and private notes

How to install

Download the compressed folder and extract its contents to a USB storage device. Launch the CarryPass.exe application and you are all set! Watch the demo videos about how to set it up and all the features on Dailymotion

The download is compatible with 64-bit Windows 10
